Our Story Through Time

Scroll to explore our company's evolution and key milestones

2010

Company Founded

Nexus Solutions was established with a vision to help businesses leverage technology for growth. Started as a small team of passionate developers working from a shared office space.

2012

First Major Project

Landed our first enterprise client, developing a comprehensive e-commerce platform that increased their online sales by 200% within the first year.

2014

Team Expansion

Grew our team to 15 professionals and moved to a larger office space. Expanded our service offerings to include mobile app development and UX design.

2016

Industry Recognition

Received the "Innovation in Technology" award at the National Business Awards for our work in developing sustainable tech solutions for small businesses.

2018

International Expansion

Opened our first international office in London to serve European clients. Partnered with leading tech firms to enhance our service capabilities.

2020

Pandemic Response

Developed remote work solutions for 50+ companies during the COVID-19 pandemic, helping them maintain operations and productivity during challenging times.

2022

AI Integration

Launched our AI and machine learning division, developing intelligent solutions for predictive analytics, customer service, and process automation.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Committed to carbon-neutral operations and launched a green tech initiative to help clients reduce their environmental impact through technology.
